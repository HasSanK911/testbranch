CERTIFICATION TRANSPARENCY

Benefits of having certificate data on the Blockchain

Data Ownership

Only you own and control your certificate data. TestBranch or no-one else owns your data.

Data Security

Your certificate data is stored securely indefinitely on the blockchain and your certificates will always be accessible.

Data Reliability

As the blockchain is the most secure way of storing your data this improves the reliability. All data can be encripted to be private or made publicly readable.

Data Encryption

The certificates are stored as an NFT (Non Fungable Token) on the blockchain. This is like a piece of data property or digital property.
