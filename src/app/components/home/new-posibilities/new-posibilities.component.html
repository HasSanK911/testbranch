New possibilities with TestBranch digital certificates

  • Enable multiple instructors or co-ordinators to work together to issue certificates

    This can be used to save time when and effort issuing certificates by enabling people who issue certificates on a single source to work together from a single account.

  • Automatically issue a final certificate after series of milestone certificates

    This can be used to reduce errors and save time when sending certificates by automating the sending of a final certificate on a course.

  • Enable the transfer of digital certificates from one person or organization to another

    Blockchain certificate ownership as NFTs allows certificates to be fully transferred to the certificate holder or organisation.
